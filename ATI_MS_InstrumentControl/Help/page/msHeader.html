Header

Description

The whole script starts with a header.
The parameters of IonTrapR0 and IonTrapZ0 define the dimension of used ion trap.
The PriRFMaxAmp is the maximum amplitude outputted from an amplifier driven by the FGen with its maximum amplitude (3.3V).

Syntax

Header( IonTrapR0, IonTrapZ0, PriRFMaxAmp )

  • IonTrapR0: A scalar between 0.01 to 20.
  • IonTrapZ0: A scalar between 0.01 to 20.
  • PriRFMaxAmp: A scalar between 0 to 100,000.
