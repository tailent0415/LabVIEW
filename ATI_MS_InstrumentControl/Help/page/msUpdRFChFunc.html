UpdRFChFunc

Description

This process updates the output function of RF channels.

Syntax

UpdRFChFunc( Ch1, Ch2, Ch3 )

  • Ch1: An integer between 0 to 9.
    0 | No output
    1 | Main RF signal (RF)
    2 | Opposite main RF signal (o-RF)
    3 | Auxiliary AC signal (AC)
    4 | Opposite Auxiliary AC signal (o-AC)
    5 | Mix of RF and AC
    6 | Mix of RF and o-AC
    7 | Mix of o-RF and AC
    8 | Mix of o-RF and o-AC
    9 | Signal obtained from function generator(see FGen)

    It define the output scale of the RF 1.

  • Ch2: An integer between 0 to 9.
    0 | No output
    1 | Main RF signal (RF)
    2 | Opposite main RF signal (o-RF)
    3 | Auxiliary AC signal (AC)
    4 | Opposite Auxiliary AC signal (o-AC)
    5 | Mix of RF and AC
    6 | Mix of RF and o-AC
    7 | Mix of o-RF and AC
    8 | Mix of o-RF and o-AC
    9 | Signal obtained from function generator(see FGen)

    It define the output function of the RF 2.

  • Ch3: An integer between 0 to 9.
    0 | No output
    1 | Main RF signal (RF)
    2 | Opposite main RF signal (o-RF)
    3 | Auxiliary AC signal (AC)
    4 | Opposite Auxiliary AC signal (o-AC)
    5 | Mix of RF and AC
    6 | Mix of RF and o-AC
    7 | Mix of o-RF and AC
    8 | Mix of o-RF and o-AC
    9 | Signal obtained from function generator(see FGen)

    It define the output function of the RF 3.
